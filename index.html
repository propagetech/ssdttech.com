Patient Community Portal

Patient community portal is a salesforce community for patients to see their medical records, scheduling surgery dates, send messages to their care team and take patient surveys etc.

Salesforce Integration

The Integration was build between Salesforce and client internal business system to sync data between two systems in real time for data consistency and leveraging salesforce features of Account, Contact, Opportunity, Products & quotes.

Salesforce Pardot & FSL

We have proven experience in implementing pardot for one of our client for determining the current status marketing activities, setting up initial templates, scoring rules etc. Field Service Lightning (FSL) is salesforce’s field service solution. FSL is an onsite job management platform with real time collaboration capabilities. We have offered FSL (Field service Lightning) solutions for one of our client.
